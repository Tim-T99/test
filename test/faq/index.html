Frequently Asked Questions
(FAQ)

FAQs are questions and answers intended to help people understand a particular subject. They address common concerns, queries, or issues that users may have about a product, service, or process, saving time for both users and businesses by providing immediate answers to recurring questions.

FAQs are commonly found on product pages under the help or support section, or dedicated FAQ sections on websites. They may be indicated by a question mark icon .

If the FAQ does not suffice in solving your problem, you can contact the entity via email, telephone, or mail. Contact details are provided in the footer section or on the 'Contact Us' page.
